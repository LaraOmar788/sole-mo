Places of enchanting beauty in Hurghada

El Gouna

El Gouna is one of the most beautiful and upscale tourist areas in Egypt, located about 25 km north of Hurghada. It is nicknamed 'Venice of Egypt' because it features artificial lakes and waterways that separate the small islands, connected by stunning bridges that add a special charm to the city.

sheraton

Sheraton Street is one of the most famous and oldest tourist streets in the city of Hurghada, and it serves as the 'downtown' for visitors of all nationalities. The street extends for a considerable length and combines popular vibes with modern appearances, making it a distinctive destination for anyone seeking enjoyment and variety..

The old market in Hurghada

A lively spot in Hurghada where you can explore spices, souvenirs, handmade crafts, and taste authentic Egyptian street life..

Mina Mosque

Hurghada Marina is a modern waterfront promenade lined with yachts, restaurants, cafés, and boutiques. It’s the perfect place for a sunset walk, fine dining, or simply relaxing by the Red Sea in a stylish, lively atmosphere.

marina

Senso Mall

Senso Mall is a contemporary shopping center offering a variety of international brands, local shops, cafes, and restaurants. It’s a great spot for fashion, gifts, or simply enjoying a relaxed shopping experience away from the crowd.

The aquarium

Hurghada Grand Aquarium is home to hundreds of marine species, interactive exhibits, and a walk-through tunnel surrounded by sharks and colorful fish. It’s an exciting and educational experience for families and ocean lovers alike

Sharm El Naqa

Sharm El Naqa is a serene beach south of Hurghada, known for its crystal-clear waters, soft sands, and vibrant coral reefs. Ideal for snorkeling, swimming, or simply relaxing in untouched natural beauty..
